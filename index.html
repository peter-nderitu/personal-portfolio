My Recipes

Chapati

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Warm water
  • 2 tbsp oil

Mix ingredients to make dough, roll into circles, fry on a hot pan, and enjoy hot!

Fries

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil

Peel, cut, and deep fry until golden. Serve with ketchup or chili!
